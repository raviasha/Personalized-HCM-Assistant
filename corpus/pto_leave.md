# Acme Corp PTO & Leave Policies

## Overview

Acme Corp is committed to supporting employees' well-being, personal obligations, and family needs through competitive paid time off and leave programs. This document covers vacation, sick leave, parental leave, and federally protected leave under the Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA).

All leave policies apply to regular full-time employees. Part-time employees (20–29 hours/week) accrue vacation and sick leave on a pro-rated basis.

---

## Paid Vacation

### Accrual Rates

Vacation time accrues each pay period (biweekly). Your annual accrual rate increases with tenure at Acme Corp:

| Years of Service | Annual Vacation Accrual |
|-----------------|------------------------|
| 0–1 year (first year) | 10 days (80 hours) |
| 1–3 years | 15 days (120 hours) |
| 3–7 years | 18 days (144 hours) |
| 7–15 years | 22 days (176 hours) |
| 15+ years | 25 days (200 hours) |

Accrual begins on your first day of employment. New hires can use accrued vacation after completing 90 days of employment.

### Vacation Carryover

Employees may carry over up to **10 days (80 hours)** of unused vacation into the following calendar year. Any vacation in excess of the carryover cap is forfeited on December 31. Acme Corp does not pay out unused vacation above the carryover cap except upon termination.

### Vacation Payout at Termination

Upon voluntary or involuntary termination, employees receive a payout for all accrued, unused vacation time (up to the carryover cap plus the current year's unused balance).

---

## Paid Sick Leave

Employees accrue **8 days (64 hours)** of paid sick leave per year, regardless of tenure, accrued biweekly.

Sick leave may be used for:
- Your own illness, injury, or preventive care
- Care for a sick or injured family member (spouse, child, parent, or domestic partner)
- Mental health appointments
- Medical appointments (doctor, dentist, specialist)

Unused sick leave carries over indefinitely but is **not paid out at termination**. Acme Corp does not count sick leave used against attendance records.

---

## Parental Leave

Acme Corp provides paid and unpaid parental leave for eligible employees following the birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child.

### Eligibility
- Regular full-time employees who have completed **6 months of continuous service**
- Leave must begin within **12 months** of the qualifying event

### Paid Parental Leave

| Role | Paid Leave Provided |
|------|---------------------|
| Primary caregiver (birth parent, or parent taking primary care role) | 16 weeks at 100% pay |
| Secondary caregiver | 6 weeks at 100% pay |

Both parents at Acme Corp may each claim their respective leave benefit simultaneously or at different times within the 12-month window.

### Unpaid Extension (FMLA Bridge)

After paid parental leave is exhausted, employees may use FMLA leave (up to 12 weeks total per year, including any FMLA leave already taken) to extend their leave unpaid with job protection.

---

## Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA)

Acme Corp complies fully with the federal FMLA. Employees who have worked at Acme Corp for at least **12 months** and at least **1,250 hours** in the past 12 months are eligible.

### Key FMLA Provisions

- Up to **12 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave** per 12-month period (rolling backward from the date leave begins)
- Up to **26 weeks** to care for a covered servicemember with a serious injury or illness
- Health insurance benefits continue during FMLA leave at the same cost to the employee
- Upon return, you are restored to the same or an equivalent position

### Qualifying Reasons for FMLA Leave

- Birth of a child and bonding within the first year (may run concurrently with paid parental leave)
- Placement of a child for adoption or foster care
- Serious health condition of the employee
- Serious health condition of a spouse, child, or parent
- Qualifying military exigency related to a family member's active duty

### Requesting FMLA Leave

Provide at least **30 days' advance notice** when the leave is foreseeable. For unanticipated leave, notify HR as soon as practicable. HR will provide the required FMLA forms; a healthcare provider must certify medical necessity within 15 days.

---

## Bereavement Leave

Acme Corp provides paid bereavement leave upon the death of a close family member:

- **Spouse or domestic partner, child, parent:** 5 paid days
- **Sibling, grandparent, grandchild, in-law:** 3 paid days
- **Other extended family or close friend:** 1 paid day (manager approval required)

Additional unpaid leave may be taken using PTO or unpaid leave.

---

## Jury Duty and Military Leave

- **Jury duty:** Full pay for up to 10 days; unpaid (or PTO use) for longer engagements
- **Military leave:** In compliance with USERRA. Up to 30 days per year paid; extended military leave unpaid with job protection

---

## Requesting Time Off

Submit PTO requests through Workday. All requests are subject to manager approval based on business needs. Please submit vacation requests at least:
- **2 weeks in advance** for absences of 1–4 days
- **4 weeks in advance** for absences of 5+ consecutive days

For sick leave or unexpected emergencies, notify your manager as early as possible.
