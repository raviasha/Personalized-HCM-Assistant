# Acme Corp Health Insurance Benefits

## Overview

Acme Corp offers comprehensive health coverage through our partnership with BlueCross BlueShield. All regular full-time employees working 30 or more hours per week are eligible for medical, dental, and vision coverage. Coverage begins on the first day of the month following your hire date (or on your hire date if you're hired on the first of the month).

---

## Medical Plan Tiers

Acme Corp offers three medical plan options to fit different needs and budgets:

### Basic Individual Plan (HDHP)

The Basic Individual Plan is a High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) designed for healthy, single employees who want lower monthly premiums.

- **Monthly premium (employee share):** $85
- **Annual deductible:** $1,600 individual
- **Out-of-pocket maximum:** $4,500 individual
- **Primary care copay (after deductible):** $30
- **Specialist copay (after deductible):** $60
- **Emergency room (after deductible):** $350
- **Prescription drugs:** Generic $15 / Brand $45 / Specialty 20% coinsurance
- **HSA-eligible:** Yes — employees on this plan may open a Health Savings Account (HSA)

### Standard Plan (PPO)

The Standard Plan is a Preferred Provider Organization (PPO) for employees who want moderate premiums with lower out-of-pocket costs at point of care.

- **Monthly premium (employee share):** $165 individual / $420 family
- **Annual deductible:** $600 individual / $1,200 family
- **Out-of-pocket maximum:** $3,000 individual / $6,000 family
- **Primary care copay:** $25 (no deductible required)
- **Specialist copay:** $50 (no deductible required)
- **Emergency room:** $250 (no deductible required)
- **Prescription drugs:** Generic $10 / Brand $35 / Specialty 15% coinsurance
- **HSA-eligible:** No — employees may use a Healthcare FSA instead

### Premium Family Plan (PPO Plus)

The Premium Family Plan provides the most comprehensive coverage with the broadest network and lowest out-of-pocket costs, ideal for employees with families or significant healthcare needs.

- **Monthly premium (employee share):** $230 individual / $590 family
- **Annual deductible:** $300 individual / $600 family
- **Out-of-pocket maximum:** $1,500 individual / $3,000 family
- **Primary care copay:** $15 (no deductible required)
- **Specialist copay:** $35 (no deductible required)
- **Emergency room:** $150 (no deductible required)
- **Prescription drugs:** Generic $5 / Brand $25 / Specialty 10% coinsurance
- **HSA-eligible:** No — employees may use a Healthcare FSA instead

---

## Dental Coverage

Acme Corp provides dental coverage through Delta Dental. All employees and their dependents are eligible.

- **Monthly premium (employee share):** $18 individual / $52 family
- **Annual benefit maximum:** $2,000 per person
- **Deductible:** $50 individual / $150 family
- **Preventive care (cleanings, X-rays):** 100% covered, no deductible
- **Basic restorative (fillings, extractions):** 80% covered after deductible
- **Major restorative (crowns, bridges):** 50% covered after deductible
- **Orthodontia:** 50% up to $1,500 lifetime maximum (children up to age 19)

---

## Vision Coverage

Vision coverage is provided through VSP (Vision Service Plan).

- **Monthly premium (employee share):** $8 individual / $20 family
- **Exam:** One exam per calendar year, $10 copay
- **Frames or contacts:** Up to $200 allowance per year
- **Lenses:** Covered in full (standard single, bifocal, trifocal) after $25 copay

## Enrollment Windows

### New Hire Enrollment
New employees have **30 days from their hire date** to enroll in health, dental, and vision coverage. If you do not enroll within 30 days, you must wait until the next Open Enrollment period unless you have a qualifying life event.

### Open Enrollment
Open Enrollment takes place **annually in November** for coverage effective January 1 of the following year. During Open Enrollment, employees may:
- Change plan tiers
- Add or remove dependents
- Enroll if previously waived coverage
- Update dental or vision elections

### Qualifying Life Events (Special Enrollment)
Outside of Open Enrollment, you may change your elections within **30 days of a qualifying life event**, including:
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Loss of other coverage (e.g., spouse loses job)
- Gain of other coverage (e.g., spouse gains job with coverage)

---

## Dependent Coverage

You may enroll your dependents, including:
- Spouse or domestic partner
- Children up to age 26 (regardless of student status or marital status)
- Children of any age if permanently disabled and dependent on you

---

## COBRA Continuation Coverage

If you lose eligibility for Acme Corp health coverage (e.g., voluntary termination, reduction in hours), you may continue coverage through COBRA for up to **18 months** (or 36 months for dependents in certain circumstances). You will pay the full premium plus a 2% administrative fee.
