# Acme Corp Life & Disability Insurance

## Overview

Acme Corp provides life insurance and disability coverage to protect employees and their families from financial hardship caused by death, serious illness, or injury. Coverage is provided through MetLife and is available to all regular full-time employees. Part-time employees working 20+ hours per week are eligible for basic coverage only.

---

## Life Insurance

### Basic Life Insurance (Company-Paid)

Acme Corp provides **basic term life insurance at no cost to employees**, with a benefit equal to **2× your annual base salary** (rounded to the nearest $1,000), up to a maximum benefit of **$500,000**.

Coverage begins on your first day of employment and requires no medical underwriting.

### Supplemental Life Insurance (Employee-Paid)

You may purchase additional life insurance for yourself, your spouse/domestic partner, or your children:

**Supplemental Employee Life:**
- Available in increments of 1× salary, up to **5× your annual base salary** (combined with basic, maximum $1,000,000)
- Rates are based on age and coverage level; premiums are deducted from your paycheck pre-tax (up to IRS limits)
- Evidence of Insurability (EOI / medical underwriting) is required for elections over **$300,000** or during non-new-hire enrollment

**Supplemental Spouse/Domestic Partner Life:**
- Available in flat amounts: $25,000 / $50,000 / $75,000 / $100,000
- EOI required for elections over $50,000

**Dependent Child Life:**
- Flat $10,000 benefit per eligible child (ages 14 days to 26 years)
- No EOI required
- One flat rate regardless of the number of children

### Naming and Updating Beneficiaries

You must designate a beneficiary for your life insurance. You can designate:
- **Primary beneficiary:** The person(s) who receives the benefit upon your death (can be split by percentage between multiple people)
- **Contingent beneficiary:** Receives the benefit only if all primary beneficiaries predecease you

**To update your beneficiary:**
1. Log in to MetLife's MyBenefits portal at mybenefits.metlife.com
2. Navigate to Life Insurance → Beneficiary Designations
3. Add, change, or remove beneficiaries and submit

> **Important:** Beneficiary designations are separate from your will. Your will does not override your beneficiary designation on file with MetLife. Review your designation after major life events (marriage, divorce, birth of a child).

### Life Events and Coverage Changes

Outside of Open Enrollment, you may adjust supplemental life insurance coverage within **30 days of a qualifying life event** (marriage, birth/adoption, loss of other coverage). EOI may be required for certain increases.

## Short-Term Disability (STD) Insurance

Short-Term Disability insurance replaces a portion of your income if you cannot work due to a covered illness, injury, or pregnancy.

### Benefit Details

- **Waiting period (elimination period):** 7 calendar days from the onset of disability
- **Benefit amount:** 70% of your weekly base salary
- **Maximum weekly benefit:** $2,500
- **Maximum duration:** 12 weeks

The first 7 days of covered disability may be bridged with your accrued sick leave. After the waiting period, STD benefits begin automatically.

### Who Pays

Acme Corp pays **100% of the STD premium** — there is no cost to employees for this coverage.

### What Is Covered

- Non-work-related illness (infections, surgery recovery, chronic conditions flaring)
- Non-work-related injury
- Pregnancy and childbirth recovery (typically 6–8 weeks, or longer with complications)
- Mental health conditions (with physician certification)

## Long-Term Disability (LTD) Insurance

Long-Term Disability insurance provides wage replacement for extended disability lasting beyond the STD benefit period.

### Benefit Details

- **Waiting period:** 90 days from the onset of disability (coordinate with STD)
- **Benefit amount:** 60% of monthly base salary
- **Maximum monthly benefit:** $10,000
- **Benefit duration:** To age 65, or Social Security Normal Retirement Age (SSNRA), whichever is later

### Definition of Disability

- **Own-occupation period (first 24 months):** You qualify if you cannot perform the material duties of your own specific job
- **Any-occupation (after 24 months):** You qualify only if you cannot perform the duties of any job for which you are reasonably suited by education, training, or experience

### Integration with Social Security Disability

LTD benefits are offset by any Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) benefits you receive. However, you keep the difference, ensuring a stable combined income.

### Optional Supplemental LTD

Employees may purchase a supplemental LTD policy that increases the benefit to **70% of monthly salary**, up to $15,000/month. Enrollment is during new-hire window or Open Enrollment; EOI may apply.

## Workers' Compensation

Work-related injuries or illnesses are covered under California Workers' Compensation insurance, separate from the disability plans above. Report any workplace injury to your manager and HR within **24 hours**. Workers' comp covers medical treatment and provides wage replacement benefits for work-related disabilities.
