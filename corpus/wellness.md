# Acme Corp Wellness Programs

## Overview

Acme Corp believes that healthy, happy employees are the foundation of our success. Our wellness programs are designed to support your physical, mental, emotional, and financial well-being. All programs are available to regular full-time and part-time employees (20+ hours/week). Dependents may participate in select programs where noted.

---

## Gym & Fitness Reimbursement

Acme Corp reimburses employees for costs related to gym memberships, fitness classes, and home gym equipment to encourage an active lifestyle.

### Reimbursement Benefit

- **Annual reimbursement maximum:** $600 per employee ($50/month equivalent)
- **Payment:** Reimbursed quarterly (March, June, September, December) via payroll
- **Eligible expenses:**
  - Gym or fitness center memberships (e.g., Planet Fitness, local YMCA, Equinox)
  - Fitness class packages (yoga, Pilates, cycling, martial arts, etc.)
  - Home fitness equipment purchases (one-time per item, max $300 per item)
  - Fitness app subscriptions (Peloton, Apple Fitness+, Nike Training Club Premium, etc.)
  - Personal trainer sessions (with licensed trainer, max $200/year)
  - Race entry fees (5K, marathon, obstacle course races, etc.)

### How to Submit

1. Log expenses in Workday under **"Wellness Reimbursement"**
2. Attach receipts (PDF or photo)
3. Submit by the last day of each quarter
4. Approved reimbursements appear in the following paycheck

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

The Employee Assistance Program provides **free, confidential** counseling and support services to employees and their household members, including:

### What's Covered (No Cost to You)

- **Short-term counseling:** Up to **8 sessions per issue per year** with a licensed counselor or therapist (in-person or virtual)
- **Mental health crisis support:** 24/7 phone access to counselors
- **Financial counseling:** Budget planning, debt management, retirement planning advice (1–3 sessions per topic per year)
- **Legal consultations:** 30-minute phone consultation with a licensed attorney (up to 3 per year); discounted extended services
- **Dependent care referrals:** Help finding childcare, elder care, or special needs resources
- **Work-life coaching:** Stress management, relationship issues, major life transitions

### How to Access

- **Phone (24/7):** 1-800-367-7474
- **Online portal:** eap.acmecorp.com (log in with your Acme Corp SSO credentials)
- All contacts are completely confidential — Acme Corp does not receive any identifying information about who uses the EAP

## Mental Health Benefits

In addition to the EAP, mental health care is covered under all Acme Corp medical plans as an in-network benefit:

- **Behavioral health copay:** $25–$35 per session (same as or lower than specialist copay, depending on plan)
- **Telehealth mental health (via Teladoc):** $0–$15 copay for video or phone sessions with licensed therapists or psychiatrists
- **Inpatient psychiatric care:** Covered after deductible at the same rate as inpatient medical care

Acme Corp has also partnered with **Calm** for a premium subscription available to all employees and dependents at no cost. Access it at calm.com/acme or via the Benefits portal.

## Annual Wellness Stipend

Employees receive a **$200 annual wellness stipend** separate from the gym reimbursement for broader wellness expenses:

**Eligible uses:**
- Standing desk or ergonomic accessories ($150 max per item)
- Blue-light blocking glasses
- Sleep tracking devices (Oura Ring, WHOOP, etc.)
- Nutrition counseling with a registered dietitian (receipts required)
- Meditation or mindfulness courses (online or in-person)
- Smoking/tobacco cessation programs

Submit via Workday under **"Annual Wellness Stipend"** — single submission per year, receipts required.

## Wellness Challenges & Rewards

Acme Corp runs quarterly **team wellness challenges** through the Virgin Pulse platform:

- Form teams of 4–6 employees
- Track steps, sleep, mindfulness, hydration, and workouts
- Top 3 teams per quarter win **Amazon gift cards** ($50 per person for 1st place, $30 for 2nd, $20 for 3rd)
- **Annual "Acme Wellness Champion"** recognition for the employee with the most challenge points in a calendar year (additional $200 gift card + recognition at All Hands)

Sign up at wellness.acmecorp.com.

## Preventive Health Benefits

All Acme Corp medical plans cover **100% of preventive care** with no copay or deductible:

- Annual physical exams
- Recommended immunizations and vaccines (including flu shots — annual on-site flu shot clinics in October)
- Cancer screenings (mammograms, colonoscopies, etc.) per USPSTF guidelines
- Prenatal care
- Well-child visits and developmental screenings

Preventive care must be from an in-network provider.

---

## Tobacco Cessation Program

Acme Corp is committed to helping employees quit tobacco. Free services include:
- Nicotine replacement therapy (patches, gum) covered 100% under all medical plans
- Prescription cessation medications (Chantix/Wellbutrin) covered after standard copay
- Coaching and behavioral support through the EAP (up to 8 sessions)
